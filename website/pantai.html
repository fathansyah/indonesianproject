Best Beach on Indonesia

Nihiwatu Beach, Sumba, Nusa Tenggara Timur

This beach is located in Nusa Tenggara Barat. Its location is hidden behind a forest. The 2.5 kilometer beach is considered as an untouched paradise, due to the beauty of the beach with white sand and the quality of the water which is still very clean and still very clean sand there.

Pink Beach, Pulau Komodo, Nusa Tenggara Timur

This pink sandy beach is clearly very unique and special. The color is pink because white sand has been mixed with red sand. This romantic sand beach has only seven in the world, and the prettiest is in Indonesia.

Pulau Weh, Aceh

This island is the westernmost point of our country, or what is often called Sabang. This very small island has three extraordinarily beautiful beaches: Anoi Itam, Gapang, and Iboih. Been there already.

Raja Ampat, Papua Barat

The beauty of Raja Ampat is famous to the world level. His charm is indeed extraordinary. If it was the western point of Indonesia, Papua became the eastern point of Indonesia. It seems no need to explain the beauty of this place.

Beach Ora, Maluku Tengah

This beach is called as Bora-Boranya Indonesia. No need to go all the way to Bora-Bora, French Polynesia, and the Maldives, Central Maluku has an island that is less beautiful than all of them.

Beach Pasir Putih, Karang Asem, Bali

Its location is hidden and away from the city crowd. Therefore, this beach is often called a hidden paradise in East Bali. As far as the eye can see it makes it cool.
