NUSA DUA LIGHT FESTIVAL 2019 "THE MOUNTAIN VIEW"

The Nusa Dua Light Festival is an annual event which has been held in Nusa Dua for the fourth time. This event was held to provide alternative entertainment attractions for tourists in the May-July period by presenting hundreds of different types of beautiful lanterns.
