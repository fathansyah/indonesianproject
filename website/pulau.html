Best Island on Indonesia

Rote Island

Known as an island located in the southernmost region of Indonesia, Rote Island in East Nusa Tenggara turns out to store beauty on its beaches. No less with the islands in the Mentawai archipelago, the beaches on Rote Island are also a great place to surf. These beaches are Nembrala Beach and Bo'a Beach.

Banda Island

Banda Island or better known as Banda Neira is one of the islands located in the Central Maluku region. This beautiful island can be reached from the city of Ambon with a trip of about 7 hours. Although quite long, but the trip is very comparable to the natural beauty is very riveting.

Misool Island

Misool Island is a beautiful island located in the Raja Ampat Islands, Papua. The beauty of the underwater world plus the blue waters that surround this island will make you even more comfortable to linger there. On this Misool island, you can do some pretty fun activities like touring the island by boat, sunbathing, scuba diving, and snorkeling.

Saparua Island

One of the charms of tourism in Maluku that you must visit is Saparua Island. The island, which is about 50 miles from Ambon, can be reached by fast boat for 1.5 hours from the Port of Tulehu, Central Maluku. On this island you will find historical relics of the heyday of the Dutch era, namely Fort Duurstede. Not only that, a variety of beaches spread out wide. Starting from white sandy beaches, such as Kulur Beach, to beaches filled with rocks, namely Tanjung Ouw.

Gili Meno Island

Gili Meno which is located in the province of West Nusa Tenggara is a suitable destination for you who are happy with a calm and natural atmosphere. The white sandy beaches are the best beaches of the dyke. The waves are very calm and the sea water is clean enough.

Derawan Island

Derawan Island, located in Berau Regency, is one of the proud islands of East Kalimantan. This island which is already quite well known among foreign countries because of the charm of the extraordinary beauty of the sea. No wonder this place is dubbed as "Tropical Nirvana in East Kalimantan".
