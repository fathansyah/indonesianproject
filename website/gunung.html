Best Mount on Indonesia

Mount kelimutu

The famous Mountain of the three crater lakes is indeed so much to attract world tourists, because the color of the lake water that can change colors. The color change itself was white, moss green, turquoise, turquoise blue, light blue, gray, red, even black. Each of these three lakes has a name, Lake named Tiwu Nuwa Muri Ko'ofai has experienced color changes 27 times since 1915, Lake Tiwu Ata Polo has changed 46 times since 1915, while Lake Ata Mbupu has changed 17 times. . According to experts, this color change is caused by mineral content in lakes and volcanic activity. Because Mount Kelimutu is indeed an active volcano. The biggest eruption of Mount Kelimutu was recorded in 1830.

Mount Ijen

This Mountain is famous among foreign tourists because of the blue bursts of fire in the crater. No wonder this is enough to attract the attention of foreign tourists, because the blaze of blue fire only exists in two locations in the world, namely in Iceland and in Ijen. Wow, cool! To watch the blue fire at Ijen, we must climb to the crater located at an altitude of 2,443 meters above sea level, and arrive at the crater at around 02.00 WIB - 03.00 WIB. It is at this time that blue flames can be seen clearly. Not only at night, in the morning we can also enjoy the beautiful sunrise which reflects its light above the turquoise crater lake water surface.

Mount Bromo

The beauty of Bromo Mountain in the eyes of the world is no doubt, as evidenced in 2017 and foreign tourists visiting Mount Bromo almost reached 23 thousand visitors. Although it is an active volcano, Bromo Mountain is among the 3 best Mountains in the world to climb without the help of porters. Not only the stunning sunrise and sunset views, the 10-square-kilometer sea of ​​sand that lies in the Bromo Mountain region is indeed one that makes Mount Bromo hard to forget.

Mount Rinjani

Mount Rinjani which is so magnificent and beautiful is already familiar to climbers, including foreign climbers. One that makes Mount Rinjani look so beautiful and majestic is because of the existence of Lake Segara Anak which is located on Mount Rinjani. The beauty of Lake Segara Anak looks more complete with the existence of Mount Batusari in the middle. Not only that, Speaking of beautiful and magnificent, Mount Rinjani does have everything. Mount Rinjani has Goa Susu stalactites and Goa Payung Mas, which is said to be the only Mount Rinjani that has Goa like this. Not only that, on Mount Rinjani found about 20 waterfalls and Savana Sembalun Lawang fields. Its popularity in foreign countries is also not in doubt, because Mount Rinjani has received several international awards, such as the World Legacy Award in the Destination Stewardship category, becoming the top 3 Tourism for Tommorow Award for the Destination Award category from the World Travel & Tourism Council. In addition, Mount Rinjani is also a Mountain with the best governance in Southeast Asia.

Mount Kerinci

Mount Kerinci is the highest active volcano in Southeast Asia. Mount Kerinci with an altitude of 3,805 meters above sea level is still so beautiful and natural. Even at the top of this Mountain we will be served by views of the crater with a truly enchanting yellowish water. While in the north, there is a beautiful and clear lake that seems unspoiled by humans, namely Lake Bento. Not only its natural beauty is a magnet for foreign tourists, but the existence of flora and fauna that is so rich and unique is also a special attraction for foreign researchers. Call it the Rafflesia Arnoldi flower, giant Suweg, Tapir, Kus-Kus, Long Tailed Monkey to the existence of short humans which until now is still a mystery about its existence.

Mount Semeru

Mount Semeru which is a favorite of both local and foreign climbers is always a Mountain that must be climbed. Mount Semeru is the highest Mountain on the island of Java, with an altitude of 3,676 meters above sea level. Many beginner climbers who start their love of climbing when visiting the Mountain which is famous for the beauty of Lake Ranu Kumbolo. But usually the journey of inexperienced climbers can only end on this lake, because the next climbing route to the Peak of Mahameru is not an easy path. Not only is the difficult terrain in the form of sand and rocks that are prone to landslides, the journey to the highest peak in Java also has other severe challenges, namely the level of the terrain is so steep, very cold temperatures and also the challenge of toxic gases. There is even a regulation that climbers must have come down from the peak before nine in the morning, because it is feared that there will be a change in wind direction that will bring poisonous gas from the active crater towards the hiking trail.
